About AeroDroneMitra

AeroDroneMitra is a smart mobile application designed to support farmers with advanced drone-based fertilizer and pesticide spraying services.

The app allows farmers to easily book professional drone vendors for efficient and precise spraying across their agricultural fields.

What We Offer

  • Drone Spraying Services: Fast and uniform spraying of fertilizers and pesticides.
  • All Crop Support: Available for paddy, cotton, maize, and more.
  • Easy Booking System: Book directly from your mobile.
